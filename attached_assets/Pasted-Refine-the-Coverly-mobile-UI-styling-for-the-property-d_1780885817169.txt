   Make chart/category colours more pastel and muted across the dashboard. Keep enough distinction between categories, but avoid bright or saturated greens/yellows/oranges unless they are warning states. Charts should feel soft, premium, and calm.

3. Add chart/ring load animation
   Add a subtle growing animation on page load for dashboard rings/charts where practical:

* Donut/ring charts should animate from 0 to their final value.
* Bar/progress visuals should softly grow/fill in.
* Keep animation quick, smooth, and non-distracting.
* Respect reduced-motion settings if already available in the app.

* Ensure correct SafeAreaView / safe-area inset handling for iPhone notch/dynamic island/status bar.
* Header title and icons must never overlap the notch/status area.

* Keep edit/back actions clear and tappable.
* Avoid making the header green-heavy.
